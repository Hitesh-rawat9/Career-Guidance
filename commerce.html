Career in Commerce & Business

Commerce careers focus on business, finance, economics, accounting, management, and entrepreneurship. This field offers diverse opportunities in corporate and financial sectors.

Career Path

  • 10+2 (Commerce or Any Stream)
  • B.Com / BBA / Economics
  • MBA / M.Com / Professional Courses

Professional Courses

  • Chartered Accountant (CA)
  • Company Secretary (CS)
  • Cost and Management Accountant (CMA)

Skills Required

  • Analytical and numerical skills
  • Communication and leadership
  • Business awareness

Top Institutions

SRCC, IIMs, DU Colleges, XLRI, NMIMS

Job Roles

Accountant, Financial Analyst, Business Consultant, Manager, Entrepreneur

Average Salary

₹4 LPA – ₹20 LPA
