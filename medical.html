Career in Medical & Healthcare

Medical and healthcare careers focus on diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases. This field requires dedication, discipline, and a strong commitment to serving society.

Career Path

  • 10+2 (PCB – Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Entrance Exams (NEET)
  • MBBS / BDS / BAMS / Nursing
  • MD / MS / Specialization (Optional)

Skills Required

  • Strong academic background in science
  • Patience and empathy
  • Decision-making skills
  • Ability to work under pressure

Top Institutions

AIIMS, JIPMER, Government Medical Colleges, Private Medical Universities

Job Roles

Doctor, Surgeon, Medical Officer, Dentist, Nurse, Medical Researcher

Average Salary

₹6 LPA – ₹25+ LPA (depending on specialization)
