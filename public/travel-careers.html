Travel & Hospitality

Create memorable experiences in tourism, events, and food & beverage industries.

Overview

The travel and hospitality industry is all about creating memorable experiences for people. From luxury hotels and airlines to adventure tourism and event management, this sector offers exciting, people-oriented careers. India's rich cultural heritage and growing tourism infrastructure make it a thriving market.
