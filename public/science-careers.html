Science & Research

Push the boundaries of knowledge through scientific discovery and innovation.

Overview

Science and research careers are for those driven by curiosity and a desire to expand human knowledge. Scientists investigate natural phenomena, develop new technologies, and solve complex problems. India offers growing opportunities in biotechnology, space research, environmental science, and pharmaceutical R&D.
