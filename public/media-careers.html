Media & Journalism

Shape public opinion and tell stories through print, digital, and broadcast media.

Overview

Media and journalism professionals inform, educate, and entertain the public. From investigative journalism and content creation to public relations and filmmaking, this field offers creative and impactful career paths. Digital media has revolutionized the industry, creating new roles and opportunities. India has a vibrant media landscape with numerous newspapers, TV channels, digital platforms, and streaming services offering diverse career options.

Career Roles in Media & Journalism

Research, investigate, and report news stories through print, digital, TV, or radio. Cover beats like politics, business, sports, or culture to inform the public.
