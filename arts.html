Career in Arts & Humanities

Arts and humanities careers focus on creativity, social sciences, languages, public services, education, and media. This field develops critical thinking and communication skills.

Career Path

  • 10+2 (Any Stream)
  • BA (Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences)
  • MA / Specialized Courses

Popular Specializations

  • English, History, Political Science
  • Psychology, Sociology
  • Journalism & Mass Communication
  • Fine Arts & Design

Skills Required

  • Creativity and imagination
  • Communication skills
  • Critical and analytical thinking

Top Institutions

Delhi University, JNU, TISS, FTII, National School of Drama

Job Roles

Teacher, Journalist, Content Writer, Psychologist, Civil Services

Average Salary

₹3 LPA – ₹12 LPA
